# FutureMUD Early Modern Writing, Print, Administration, and Finance Design Reference

## Scope

This branch extends the shared writing and minimum print catalogue into the larger Early Modern postal, legal, commercial, financial, publishing, and archival ecology.

## Reuse boundary

Reuse the 39 `preindustrial_writing_*` aliases and the shared hand press, type case, composing stick, chase, inking balls, drying rack, broadside, pamphlet, almanac, blank form, and printed map sheet. Do not create era-prefixed copies of those forms.

## Planned slices

- newspapers, gazettes, sermons, proclamations, playbills, tickets, journals, natural-philosophy papers, atlases, music sheets, catalogues, and distinct printed-book formats;
- folded-letter packets, postal bags, courier satchels, deed boxes, filing bundles, dispatch packets, archive shelving, and seal/security goods;
- daybooks, ship logs, port/customs registers, plantation/company/notary ledgers, briefs, warrants, passes, passports, indentures, contracts, bills of lading, bills of exchange, insurance policies, and proto-share papers;
- inkstands, sand shakers, penknives, sealing-wax goods, portable writing desks, clerk kits, and office furniture;
- type sorts, punches/matrices, formes, press furniture, paper reams, bookshop shelving, pamphlet racks, and distribution stock.

## Dependency contract

Paper, parchment, oak, leather, linen, brass, lead, type metal, printing ink, and common writing/tool tags are live. Papermaking, Bookbinding, Scribing, Calligraphy, Woodblock Printing, Movable Type Printing/Printer, and Engraving/Engraver skills exist. Administration and Law remain optional professional-skill choices and must not be hard prerequisites without installer checks.

Readable/writeable/fixed-content component decisions must precede claims that printed documents carry mutable or preset text.

## Exclusions and acceptance

Exclude typewriters, carbon paper, staples, paperclips, binders, modern file folders, fountain pens, ballpoints, and modern postal stationery.

Every new row must differ from shared stock by format, component, institution, production technology, or gameplay role; public descriptions must not claim postal, financial, or security behaviour that the components do not supply.
